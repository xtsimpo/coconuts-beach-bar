The Island

Discover
Kythnos

One of the Cyclades' most authentic gems, close enough for a weekend, beautiful enough to stay forever.

Chora & Dryopida

Whitewashed alleys, bougainvillea, and kafeneions that feel frozen in the best kind of time.

Loutra Thermal Springs

Healing mineral waters flowing since antiquity. Europe's oldest spa destination still works its magic.

Kolona Beach

The most iconic & famous beach, characterized by a narrow strip of sand that connects the mainland to the islet of Agios Loukas.

Katafyki Cave

One of the largest and most impressive caves in Greece, located in the village of Dryopida in central Kythnos.

Panagia Kanala

Panagia Kanala is the spiritual heart of Kythnos, a sacred pilgrimage site perched on a pine-covered cape in the island's southeastern region.

Over 90 Beaches

From the iconic Kolona double-beach to Paralia Skylou & Gaidouromantra, a different paradise for every mood.
